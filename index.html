What is Colon Cancer?

Colon cancer occurs when cells in the large intestine grow uncontrollably. Changes in gene expression can reveal how cancer cells differ from healthy cells.

Gene Expression

Gene expression measures how active different genes are. By comparing cancer and healthy samples, we can find genes that are turned up or down.

Microarrays

Microarrays measure thousands of genes at once by detecting fluorescence intensity from DNA probes.
